TROI Achievements

Paragliding Tourism in Puncak Bogor

Paragliding Tourism in Nusa Dua
Recently there are 500 persons/day or 15.000 persons/months visit Nusa Dua Paragliding Site, Nusa Dua, Bali for Paragliding Flight.
MURI Record Breaking for Highest Flag Raising with Paramotor
Highest Indonesian Flag Raising Ceremony with Paramotor on 2020 meter height in Atang Sanjaya AFB, Bogor, West Java (17 August 2020).
Rafting Tourism in Citarik, Sukabumi
Recently there are 36.000 persons/year enjoying rafting in Citarik, Sukabumi, West Java, up to 500 locals participated in this activities (90% are nearby residents)
